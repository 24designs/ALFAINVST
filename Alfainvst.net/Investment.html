Investment Types

HNWI comprises investors with diverse investment needs and nuances. These differences can only be catered to by having different investment types which include among others

  • Co-investments with other investment companies
  • Single strategy investment
  • Fund of funds
  • Indirect investments

Investment Strategy

ALFAINVST’s investment strategy was developed by our founding directors. This strategy is the foundation of success and comprises of

  • Structured selection process
  • Clear direction from directors
  • Dedicated professional teams to source and manage
  • Contact leveraging
  • Integrity and zero corruption compliance

Risk Management

“The risk of loss resulting from inadequate or failed internal processes, people and systems or from external events.”

ALFAINVST’s investment risk team is headed by the Risk Management Director and comprises of 8 member staff who work across with Investment Managers. ALFAINVST provides our investors with a structured risk management system that includes :-

Compliance to legal and regulatory requirements.

Implementing controls to safeguard against risk.

Strong management and succession plan implementation.

Entrepreneurial leadership setting clear mission, vision and objectives.

Clear and measurable investment policy.

Due diligence for complex high risk structures

Private equity investment

ALFAINVST’s private equity investment is structured as private equity partnerships comprising of general partner(s) and limited partners. This partnership agreement are tailor made to the needs of the limited partners, which defines the role of the signatories, investment period, divestment strategy, monitoring periods and key performance indicators.

ALFAINVST’s private equity investments have consistently returned return of investments of minimum 4 points above market average.
